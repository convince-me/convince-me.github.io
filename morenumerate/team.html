Our Team

This research is led by Michael Ranney at the University of California at Berkeley. Collaborators on this work include many of his current and former students (including L. Rinne, L. Miratrix, M. Song, A. Galpern, M. Crain) and Ed Munnich at the University of San Francisco.

Collaborators on the application of this work to journalism education include Louise Yarnall and Patricia Schank at the Center for Technology in Learning at SRI International, Tom Johnson at the Institute for Analytic Journalism, and Orville Schell and Cynthia Gorney at the Graduate School of Journalism at UC Berkeley.
